WINGS OF FIRE

"Wings of Fire" is a popular fantasy book series by Tui T. Sutherland that follows the adventures of young dragons in a world divided by seven dragon tribes: MudWings, SandWings, SkyWings, SeaWings, RainWings, IceWings, and NightWings. The series begins with a prophecy that foretells five dragonets, known as the Dragonets of Destiny, who are meant to end a war that has raged for decades over the SandWing throne. These dragonets—Clay, Tsunami, Glory, Starflight, and Sunny—are raised in secret and eventually escape to forge their own paths, navigating a world of alliances, betrayals, and secrets. The series explores themes of destiny, friendship, and the complex nature of good and evil, with each arc focusing on different characters and tribes, expanding the lore and depth of the dragon world while delving into issues like prejudice, leadership, and identity. Across its multiple arcs, "Wings of Fire" balances intense action with deep character development, drawing readers into a rich, complex world where dragons are the heroes, villains, and everything in between.
