Wimpy Kid

Diary of a Wimpy Kid by Jeff Kinney follows Greg Heffley, a middle schooler navigating the trials of adolescence. The story is presented as Greg's diary, filled with his thoughts, doodles, and observations about his daily life. Greg sees himself as destined for greatness, but his reality is filled with awkward social interactions, family dynamics, and school challenges. He often schemes to gain popularity, avoid embarrassment, and deal with his clueless parents, troublemaking older brother Rodrick, and sweet but annoying younger brother Manny. Greg’s best friend, Rowley Jefferson, is his loyal companion, though their friendship experiences ups and downs as Greg's self-centered nature causes friction. The series humorously captures the struggles of growing up, with Greg’s misadventures highlighting his flaws and often leading to unintended consequences, making him both relatable and entertaining to readers.
